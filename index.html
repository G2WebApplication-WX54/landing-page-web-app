We are an independent company formed by 5 students from the Universidad Peruana de Ciencias Aplicadas, who have created a web application to guide users through safe routes and alert them about risk areas in real-time, enhancing personal safety in Peru.

PeaceApp


Discover PeaceApp: your guide to moving safely around the city. Access safe routes and real-time risk area alerts, all in an intuitive app designed to keep you informed and protected.

Our Services

Interactive Heat Map:

Visualize high and low-risk areas with real-time updates.

Safe Routes:

Receive recommendations for safe paths based on your location and destination.

Community Reports:

Contribute and consult security incident reports, supporting collaborative vigilance.

Our Plans

Standard Plan

Access the platform and unlimited use of the real-time heat map, ideal for citizens committed to their safety and that of their community. [Free]

Governmental Plan

Designed for public entities, this plan offers privileged access to advanced statistics, detailed incident reports, and the ability to send mass security notifications, facilitating proactive management and response to crime. [Subscription]

About the Product

PeaceApp is designed to help you navigate the city safely. With real-time updates on risk areas and safe routes, our app ensures you stay informed and protected wherever you go. Explore the features of PeaceApp through our informative video.
