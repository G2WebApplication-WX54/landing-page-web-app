We are an independent company formed by 5 students from the Universidad Peruana de Ciencias Aplicadas, who have created a web application to guide users through safe routes and alert about risky areas in real-time, improving personal security in Peru.

PeaceApp


Discover PeaceApp: your guide to moving safely through the city. Access safe routes and alerts of risky areas in real-time, all in an intuitive application designed to keep you informed and protected.

Our Services

Interactive Heat Map:

Visualize high and low-risk areas with real-time updates.

Safe Routes:

Receive recommendations for safe paths based on your location and destination.

Community Reports:

Contribute to and consult security incident reports, supporting collaborative surveillance.

Our Plans

Standard Plan

Access to the platform and unlimited use of the real-time heat map, ideal for citizens committed to their safety and that of their community. [Free]

Government Plan

Designed for public entities, this plan offers privileged access to advanced statistics, detailed incident reports, and the ability to send mass security notifications, facilitating proactive management and response to crime. [Subscription]
